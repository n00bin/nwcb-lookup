How it works: Add only the insignias that aren't yet Celestial Account-Bound. Each entry has a rarity pill and a quantity. When you upgrade one in-game, click the ↑ next to that insignia to bump that copy up the rarity ladder (Epic → Legendary → Mythic → Celestial). When you upgrade a Celestial copy to Celestial Account-Bound, click ↑ once more — that copy graduates and is removed from tracking. Then you can 1-for-1 swap it onto every other character that has the same insignia, and click ↑ on each of their Celestial copies too as you do.
