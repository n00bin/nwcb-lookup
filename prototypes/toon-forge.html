Tell us about your toon

Paragon, race, item level. The three knobs that decide what your stats actually mean.

Ability scores

Class hands you a base. L9 and L16 sprinkle +1 on everyone for free. The other six levels each let you pick two stats and bump them by +1. Gear and boons stack on top automatically.

Equip your gear

Click any slot. The pickers know which class wears what. Empty slots are just wasted Item Level.

Enchantments

Eleven slots, three flavors. 4 Offense, 4 Defense, 1 Utility, 1 Combat, 1 Bonus. Universal enchants morph their effect to the slot type they sit in.

Overloads

Two slots, each a flat percent buff. Pick the same one twice and the dropdown will quietly hide it from you.

Artifacts

Primary's combat power is what you press in fights. The other three sit pretty for stat bonuses.

Boons

Spend your campaign points. Tap the +/− on each row. Master tier costs more per rank but stacks all three effects.

Guild boons

One pick per category — whatever your guild's stronghold has unlocked. Offense, Defense, Utility.

Optional buffs

Stuff you eat, drink, or smash before a fight. One per category. Always-on freebies sit at the bottom — uncheck the ones you don't actually have.

Run the math

Pick a power magnitude. The math uses the universal NW PvE enemy: 50% Defense, 50% Deflect, 90% Deflect Severity, and 0% Awareness / Crit Avoid / Accuracy. Your Accuracy counters their Deflect Severity.
